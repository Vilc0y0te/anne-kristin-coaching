About

Curating visions. Navigating change.

Jungian Coach, Creative Thinker & Founder of The Big WHY Podcast

From the corporate script to authoring my own story

Like many, I grew up with dreams. But I was encouraged to leave them behind and focus on the "real world." I followed the societal script, building a successful corporate career in advertising, cultural management, and luxury retail. From the outside, everything looked great, but on the inside, I felt disconnected from my true self.

After hitting rock bottom, I realized my life wasn't aligned with who I truly am or what I wanted to contribute to the world. I left my job to pursue my passion for art, a dream I had buried for too long. I moved to Italy to study children's book illustration at the Accademia di Belle Arti in Macerata.

While I loved my new path, the journey taught me something essential: success isn't one-size-fits-all. Each person must find their own path, and that journey begins with a shift in mindset and a curated vision for the future.

In 2020, I became a certified coach and turned everything I had learned (through art, through Jungian psychology, through my own transformation) into a practice that helps others break free from societal expectations and become the authors of their own stories.
