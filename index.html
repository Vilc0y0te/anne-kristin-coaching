Questions

Frequently asked questions

If you're feeling stuck, uncertain about a decision, or sensing a gap between where you are and where you want to be, coaching can help. You don't need to have everything figured out before you start. That's what the discovery call is for: we'll talk through where you are and whether my approach is a good fit.

Therapy often focuses on healing past wounds and mental health conditions. Coaching focuses on the present and future: helping you gain clarity, make better decisions, and take action toward the life you want. Both are valuable, and they can complement each other. If I sense therapy might be more appropriate, I'll say so honestly.

All sessions happen over video call, so location doesn't matter. I work with clients across Europe, Asia, the US, and beyond. Sessions are available in English, German, and Italian.

Life happens. You can reschedule with 24 hours' notice at no charge. I keep things flexible because rigidity is usually part of the problem, not the solution.

The Deep Dive program and Single Clarity Session have different investment levels. Exact pricing is shared during the free discovery call, where we can also discuss payment plans if needed. I want the fit to be right, for both of us.

Ready to stop waiting?

The discovery call is free, pressure-free, and 30 minutes. We'll talk about where you are, where you want to be, and whether working together makes sense.
