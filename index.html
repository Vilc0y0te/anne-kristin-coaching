Frequently asked questions

If you're feeling stuck, uncertain about a decision, or sensing a gap between where you are and where you want to be, coaching can help. That's what the discovery call is for: we'll talk through where you are and whether my approach is a good fit.

Therapy often focuses on healing past wounds and mental health conditions. Coaching focuses on the present and future: helping you gain clarity, make better decisions, and take action toward the life you want.

All sessions happen over video call, so location doesn't matter. I work with clients across Europe, Asia, the US, and beyond. Sessions are available in English, German, and Italian.

You can reschedule with 24 hours' notice at no charge. I keep things flexible because rigidity is usually part of the problem, not the solution.
