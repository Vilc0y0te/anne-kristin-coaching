Once upon a time, there was an ugly duckling who felt different, misunderstood, and strangely misaligned — until one day it discovered its true nature. It had never been a duck at all, but a beautiful swan. In that moment, what once felt like quiet longing turned into a fairy tale — and that's where the real story of the swan began.

Until we truly know who we are, we may live a life that was never meant for us — guided by forces we sense but do not yet understand. Beneath the surface of our lives, invisible currents are always at work — shaping our choices, repeating old patterns, guiding us along paths we never consciously chose. When left in the shadows, they quietly run the show. But once brought into awareness, something profound begins to shift: these very forces turn into allies. What once felt confusing or overwhelming often holds the seed of our greatest strength — hidden gifts waiting to be recognised and claimed.

My work invites you into a deeper dialogue with these inner archetypal energies so you can move from unconscious patterns to conscious authorship, and step into the story you are truly here to live.

Meaning & Mystery

We are born into a world we do not fully understand, and from the beginning, we try to make sense of what we see, feel, and experience. Yet if the veil of mystery were ever lifted completely, life might become strangely flat — stripped of wonder, depth, and enchantment. My work is to help you find meaning in the everyday while keeping that sense of mystery alive.

Frequently asked questions

How do I know if coaching is right for me? ⌄

If you're feeling stuck, uncertain about a decision, or sensing a gap between where you are and where you want to be, coaching can help. Send me a message and we'll talk through where you are and whether my approach is a good fit.

What makes this different from traditional coaching? ⌄

This work goes deeper than goal-setting and accountability. By combining Jungian depth psychology, narrative coaching, active imagination, and applied semiotics, we access the unconscious patterns that shape your decisions.

How do sessions work? ⌄

All sessions happen over video call, so location doesn't matter. I work with clients across Europe, Asia, the US, and beyond. Sessions are available in English, German, and Italian. You can reschedule with 24 hours' notice at no charge.
