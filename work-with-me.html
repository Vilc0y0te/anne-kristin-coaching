How I Work

My Method

My method is shaped by my love for art, stories, and the deeper layers of the human psyche. As a Jungian-oriented coach, I explore questions of personal identity, inner narrative, and purpose.

My work moves beyond surface-level conversation into the deeper realms of the subconscious. Art — whether images, stories, or music — evokes emotion, and emotion is the language of the unconscious. Long before written language, human beings made sense of their world through symbols. That symbolic capacity still lives within us.

I value science and rational thought. Yet through my study of Carl Jung and depth psychology, I came to see that our modern emphasis on logic has overshadowed something equally essential: our ability to trust instinct, read symbols, and feel connected to a larger field of meaning.

In my work, I draw on ancient wisdom traditions — including mythology, symbolic thinking, and early animistic worldviews — not as superstition, but as psychological insight into how human beings have always created meaning.

Alongside reflective enquiry, I work with mythology, art, dreams, and archetypal imagery. By engaging with symbols that live deep in the unconscious, we can access insights that help you reconnect with your authentic self and move forward with greater clarity, purpose, and alignment.

Archetypal Reading

Understand the forces shaping your life

What are archetypes?

Archetypes are universal inner patterns that shape the way we feel, act, and relate to the world. Each of us carries a unique constellation of these forces within us, and we draw on them in different moments of life: caring for a child, we may embody the Caregiver; pitching an idea, the Warrior; stepping into unfamiliar territory, the Explorer.

To understand your archetypal mix is to understand yourself more deeply — why you move through life as you do, where your natural strengths lie, what kind of work or way of life is truly aligned with you, and which inner gift is waiting to be claimed. It also helps illuminate the tensions within: the conflicting forces that may pull you in different directions or keep certain parts of you hidden.

This method combines reflective enquiry, story exploration, association tests, and coaching with artworks.

The exploration lasts around 1.5 hours. Afterwards, you will receive a detailed Archetypal Map, an Archetypal Snapshot designed as a card, and a Fantasy Portrait of your favourite inner archetype — a lasting visual reminder of the insight revealed in the session.
